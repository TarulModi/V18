Advance Payment/Register Payment From Sales and Purchase orders

You can register advance payment from sales quotation and purchase quotation. This application generate payments with proper journal entry which you can reconcile with the invoice/bills from same customer/vendors. it will maintain advance payment history also in respective documents.

Module Features
  • Register Advance Payment from Sales Quotations and Purchase Quotations.
  • Maintain Advance Payment History in Particular Document.
  • User can check received or paid advance amount in sales quotation and purchase quotations tree view.
  • User can check balance amount in sales quotation and purchase quotations in tree view.
  • user can also select payment state either draft or posted.
  • 50 days free support

Sales Quotation Advance Payment Flow.

First Of all create Sales Quotation. You can see Advance Payment Button in only Draft State.

When You click on Advance Payment, It will Open One wizard to fill some details. you can register advance payment in different currency and you can also define payment state either draft or posted.

After Register Advance Payment, you can see 1 smart button that will redirect to that payment. If you dont want to redirect to that payment then also you can see advance payment history lines.

You can see the journal entry for that registered advance payment.

In sales quotation tree view, you can see received advance payment amount and balance amount.
