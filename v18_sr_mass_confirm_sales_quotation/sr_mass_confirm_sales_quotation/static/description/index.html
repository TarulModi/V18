Mass Confirm Sales Quotation and Send Email

This application will help you to confirm multiple quotations with send email to the customers at a time. You will also have option to select email template in wizard.

Video Tutorial Contact Us
Module Features
  • Multiple confirm quotation at a time
  • Multiple send emails to the customer at a time
  • Option to select email template
  • 50 days Free support

Select multiple quotations and Go to the action menu and select confirm quotations with email menu.

One wizard will appear when you click on that menu. If you dont want to send email then just click on confirm order button.

If you want to send email at the time of confirm quotation, simply just click on that check box and you will have option to select email template.

After clicking on that button, you can see all that selected order is confirmed.

If you have selected send mail option with template, automatically execute emails with respective customers.
